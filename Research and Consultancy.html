Research

Today, in India, a prominent gap exists between research carried out at academic institutions and research required by the industry. This is because of a lack of dialogue between academia and industry where academic research is restricted to publishing papers and dissertations while industry research is oriented towards Patent and commercialization opportunities.

These potholes result in limited opportunities for graduates in biological sciences. Therefore, the majority of these students opt for higher education abroad or take up whatever job that is available.

However, we sincerely believe that Biological Sciences have a huge potential to stand on their own and contribute to the development of the society. But, to achieve the same, our students need to be trained and guided at the basic level of education, where they are encouraged to take up applied research that has market value, and, they are prepared to create opportunities rather than looking for one.

This indicates an urgent need to commence conversation between Industry and Academia to align and synchronize towards defining goals and work together for achieving the same. We, therefore, think that it can best be done with prospective collaboration between academia and industry. In this direction, we take it upon ourselves as an industry partner to help and develop high quality scientific as well as commercial outlook in the institutions and students, which can go a long way to come, fulfilling the goals of becoming ATMANIRBHAR Bharat.

Scientific Branding

In the modern times, people understand and value a thing if it's backed by science. Many products in spite of having a huge potential fail to capture markets because of lack of knowledge amongst people.
