CATTLE FEED BUSINESS

Approach, need, about concentrated feeds, science behind product range

An ideal cattle feed consisting of hay, pelleted feed, and grains provide energy, protein, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins ,minerals, fibers and water in appropriate amounts. A balanced combination of all these components is important to get maximum milk productivity. Any component which is lacking or available in lesser quantities becomes limiting to the production of milk resulting in reduced productivity.

Animal Bioscience

Cattle feed development and manufacturing

BoviBooster

BoviBooster provides high quality protein, fat, energy, required amino acids, minerals and vitamins to fulfill the basic needs of cattle during the dry or prepartum period at an economic cost. Moreover, regular feeding of BoviBooster in this period ensures delivery of a healthy calf.

NutriBooster

NutriBooster can be started after calving and can be continued upto 6 months through the peak production period. It helps achieve the highest milk production thus increasing the overall productivity of the cattle.NutriBooster also contains a blend of probiotics.

BoosterPlus

BoosterPlus is the product of choice added with vitamins and minerals and (bypass) fat and bypass protein, BoosterPlus also ensures digestion due to its unique probiotic formula. Use of BoosterPlus ensures extended milking capacity at this time thus helping getting more milk out of the animal for longer periods.
